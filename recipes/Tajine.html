Tajine

Description

Tunisian Tajine is a baked dish similar to a frittata, made with eggs, cheese, meat, and spices. It is a staple dish in Tunisia, often served as an appetizer or main course, rich in flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup grated cheese (Gruyère or Parmesan)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the cooked chicken or ground beef, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the mashed potatoes, cheese, and chopped parsley.
  5. Combine the egg mixture with the cooked meat and stir well.
  6. Grease a baking dish and pour in the mixture, smoothing the top.
  7. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until golden and firm.
  8. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
